Award Classifications

Categories & Available Honors

Nominations span three geographic categories. All five honors are available within each category.

Category A — National: Open to alumni of Indian origin currently working within India.
Category B — International (Indian Origin): Open to alumni of Indian origin currently working abroad.
Category C — International: Open to international alumni (non-Indian origin) currently working abroad.
